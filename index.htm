About Independence Day

On August 14, 1947, Pakistan gained independence from British rule. It is a day of national pride and a reminder of the sacrifices made by our forefathers. Let us celebrate the spirit of freedom and unity that defines our nation.

How to Celebrate

Flag Hoisting

Participate in flag hoisting ceremonies and display the national flag with pride.

Community Events

Join local events and parades to celebrate with fellow citizens.

Traditional Foods

Enjoy traditional Pakistani foods and sweets with your family and friends.
